The move isn't one decision — it's the same fifteen decisions asked of every place. This briefcase holds the fifteen so no candidate gets graded on charm instead of fit. A town can be beautiful on a Saturday and wrong for a life.

The non-negotiable up top: Maddy's support services. Only two states clear the bar — California (Lanterman Act entitlement) and Oregon (K-Plan, no waitlist). Every destination in this book lives in one of those two. Everything else is a comparison, not a gate.

01  ·  The Fifteen — what each place is graded on
01
Regional Center & DDS
Which regional center serves the county; office distance; whether eligibility transfers or restarts.Decisive. Everything else is negotiable; this isn't.
02
Healthcare access
Nearest hospital, specialists, in-network coverage, ER drive time.A household of five with real history can't be an hour from care.
03
Housing — rental
A 3bd/2ba minimum under $3,000/mo — and whether that band actually exists in the local market.The number that has to work is the rent, not the list price.
04
LGBTQ+ community
Visible community, safety, Pride presence — neighbors, not novelties.Not just legal — lived. It has to feel like ours.
05
Clean eating & grocery
Co-op or grocer carrying organic, wild-caught, pasture-raised, non-GMO.The standard doesn't relax for zip code.
06
Strength training
A serious gym — real iron, 24-hr access, walkable if possible.Daily infrastructure, not a nice-to-have.
07
Fly fishing & water
Tailwater / river / access points within reach; season length.The thing that makes a place feel like home, not just housing.
08
Dog & trails
Off-leash options, dog-friendly walkable trails for Cooper.70 lbs of Bernedoodle votes too.
09
Climate & environment
Heat load, air quality, seasonal feel, elevation.The weather you actually live in, not the brochure day.
10
Disaster & insurance
Wildfire exposure, flood, insurability, premium reality.In California this is a cost line, not a footnote.
11
Connectivity & travel
Broadband for remote work; nearest airport; drive to a real city.The income is remote — the pipe and the runway have to be there.
12
Taxes & cost of living
State tax posture, overall CoL vs. national, entitlement-law tradeoffs.CA costs more; the entitlement is what we're buying.
13
Support proximity
Distance to Karston (SF), existing friends, chosen family, backup.Connection is foundational — don't move away from the net.
14
Neighborhoods
The specific streets — forever-home lane vs. charm-you-visit zone.You don't move to a town, you move to a block.
15
Vibe & walkability
Downtown feel, third places, whether daily life happens on foot.The intangible that decides if you stay after year one.
